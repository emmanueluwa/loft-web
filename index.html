About Gina

I'm Gina, owner of The Grooming Loft.

I discovered the Pet Grooming industry as a volunteer 6 years ago, ever since then I have been dedicated to pet grooming as a career.

In those 6 years I worked for a small business, starting from learning the basics to progressing to Salon Manager. Along the way I have learnt all the knowledge and skills within the industry that I know today.

I am dedicated to my passion of animal care, providing a calm environment and positive experience for all.

No pet is too big or too small, I welcome all! So please get in touch, I can't wait to start grooming your fluffy friends.

Emmi-pet Teeth Cleaning

I offer advanced Emmi-pet teeth cleaning to keep that bite pearly white.

The non-invasive, ultrasonic technology gently cleans the teeth without the need for anaesthesia. No more dreaded trips to the vet when we can help maintain healthy teeth and gums here at The Grooming Loft.
